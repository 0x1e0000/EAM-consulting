Regional Potential Analysis

North Africa makes successful progress in establishing industries. Investors rely on broad support, geographic location, political stability and favorable wages. Morocco is increasingly becoming a hub for the (Western) African market. North Africa offers many opportunities for foreign companies.

The companies that are already on the ground in North Africa have also achieved good results. We assist with analytics to find the right location for your business and provide marketing concepts.

Cluster & Network Development

We establish contacts with the relevant government members, ministries, political partners and institutions in order to secure investment projects as well as investment project subsidies of the state and to favorably influence decision-making (lobbying). We offer our broad and quick-to-use network to you

Trade Promotion

Africa continues to be one of the fastest growing regions in the world. High growth rates, increasing investments from abroad, Industries developing dynamically, even outside the raw materials sector, as well as overall improved general conditions create great economic potential for foreign companies.
