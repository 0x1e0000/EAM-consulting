Define responsabilities

Your project has top priority. As an executive sponsor, I am solely responsible for the success of the project. A governance structure with clear roles and responsibilities will be defined and agreed. Decision makers of the right levels of leadership will be appointed to the Steering Committee.

Develop a business case

Whether your project has the goal of solving a problem or unlocking business potential, a well-defined and sustainable business case and quantifiable value are needed. This is important for the investment decision, and an essential motivating factor for the actors.

Determine a project scope

With you the project scope is defined clearly and in detail as possible. We make sure that everyone involved knows what the result should be. Changes during implementation are likely to cause delays, so early on we define the process that will later control changes in scope.

Set dates.

Time is money. This intent applies to projects always and unrestricted. With a completion date, there is a high likelihood that there will be no delays, no unnecessary direct costs, no lost revenues, no lost benefits and no impact on the business case.

Plan Budget.

We reliably estimate the budget with you, guided by the scope of the project and the urgency. We agree with you test cycle, in which you control the project status and budget consumption.

Ensure Quality.

Together with you, we define the quality criteria for your project and ensure that the expected quality standards or projects are met.
